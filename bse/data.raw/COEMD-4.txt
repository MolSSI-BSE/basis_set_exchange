FROM DESCRIPTION:
----------------
The coemd-n basis sets are parametrized for computing the moments of the electron momentum density at the SCF level of theory at a 10^-n accuracy with respect to the estimated CBS limit, produced by the coemd-ref set.
The reference for the basis sets is
J. Lehtola, P. Manninen, M. Hakala and K. H&#228;m&#228;l&#228;inen, Completeness-optimized basis sets. Application to ground-state electron momentum densities, J. Chem. Phys. 137, 104105 (2012).
