NLO-V valence double-zeta polarized basis set developed for nonlinear optics calculation, specially for first hiperpolarizability (beta) prediction.
Below, we presented the average absolute deviation for first hyperpolarizability (beta) for a total of 69 organic push-pull molecules calculated at cam-B3LYP/NLO-V and cam-B3LYP/6-31G(2d) levels.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
First Hyperpolarizability                       Average Absolute Deviation
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                               NLO-V              6-31G(2d)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Beta values between 0-20 esu                    7.2                 8.8
Beta values between 21-100 esu                  22.0                33.2
Beta values between 101-190 esu                 18.8                146.3
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
We conclude that the new basis set (NLO-V), together with cam-B3LYP functional, make an affordable calculation scheme to predict NLO properties of large organic molecules.
