ROHF Energies (Hartree) obtained with NWChem 5.1.1
obtained with standard 3-21G and 6-31G basis sets,
and improved 6-31G (m6-31G, m6-31G*) basis sets
(J. Chem. Phys. 2003, 118, 7775-7782)
NB.1
----
Note that these energies are for Sc, Fe, Co, Ni
slightly different than those reported in the m6-31G
paper. This is most likely due to NWChem vs. ATOMSCF,
i.e. &quot;molecular&quot; states vs. atomic states.
NB.2
----
The m6-31G and m6-31G* basis for Zn are the same as
the 6-31G and 6-31G* basis sets.
